Safety, Ethics and Privacy

Community governance layer for MakerPlan

Overview

This system handles real community carpenter data and product ideas. As a community-facing tool we have a responsibility to protect privacy, ensure fair access, and prevent misuse of AI-generated build plans.

The following policies were co-created with community carpenters and workshop stakeholders as part of our Living Lab governance framework.

Identified Risks and Severity

High

Unsafe Build Instructions

AI may generate build steps that skip safety precautions for power tools, sharp edges, or structural loads.

High

Budget Misrepresentation

ML cost predictions may not reflect real local timber and material prices in the carpenter's area.

Medium

Skill Level Mismatch

Plans generated for an incorrect skill level could lead to failed builds or dangerous use of tools.

Medium

Data Privacy

Carpenter product ideas and business plans submitted to the system could be exposed to competitors.

Low

Language Barriers

Non-English speaking carpenters may struggle to follow AI-generated plans written in English only.

Low

Access Inequality

Carpenters without internet access or devices cannot use the system without workshop assistance.

Carpenter Consent Agreement

I consent to my build idea and constraints being stored for plan generation purposes only.
I understand that the AI-generated prototype plan is a recommendation and must be verified before building.
I agree that my data will not be shared outside of this carpentry workshop.
I acknowledge that I can submit feedback to revise any plan I believe is unrealistic or unsafe.

Safeguards In Place

Carpenter feedback loop allows any unsafe or unrealistic plan to be flagged and revised by the AI
ML feasibility predictor flags builds unlikely to succeed before a plan is generated
Cost predictions are grounded in 600 real synthetic records based on local South African timber pricing
All data is stored locally and is not shared with external servers or third parties
Carpenters can print their plans for offline use in workshops with limited internet access
Skill level is captured upfront to ensure plans and tool recommendations match the carpenter's actual capability

Living Lab AI Charter

Transparency

Carpenters can always see how their plan was generated and what ML predictions were used.

Fairness

All carpenters receive equal access to the planning system regardless of background or skill level.

Privacy

Only build-related data is collected. No personal identifiers beyond the carpenter's name.

Accountability

Carpenters are responsible for verifying plans and applying proper safety precautions before building.

Community Control

The workshop administrator can override or delete any AI-generated plan at any time.

Iteration

The system improves through real carpenter feedback across multiple Living Lab cycles.
